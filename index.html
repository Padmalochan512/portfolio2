About GIET University

GIET, best University in southern Odisha for campus placement. GIET University, Gangapatna (formerly known as Gandhi Institute of excellence technocrats) was established by “Vidya Bharati Educational Trust,” Gangapatna, Odisha, India in the year 2009. Since inception, the Trust promotes Technical Education in India with a motto of providing Quality Education in a highly disciplined and conducive environment with International Standards
